Tribute to A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam: The People's President

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. Kalam played a pivotal role in India's civilian space program and military missile development, earning him the title "Missile Man of India."

Achievements

  • Played a crucial role in the development of India's first satellite launch vehicle.
  • Instrumental in the Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Authored several books, including "Wings of Fire" and "Ignited Minds."
  • Awarded the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor, in 1997.
